About Me

Hello there! I'm Racheline Mouyana, a 22-year-old front-end developer originally from the beautiful city of Cape Town, South Africa. In 2020, I graduated high school and embarked on a journey to merge my passion for technology with my love for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. Alongside my pursuits in web development, I also have the incredible opportunity to teach English virtually to people from all corners of the globe. This unique blend of cultural experiences and language instruction fuels my creativity and understanding of user needs. I am dedicated to crafting seamless and engaging digital experiences, and I'm excited to contribute my skills to the ever-evolving world of front-end development. Let's build something amazing together!
